"The Forty-six Thousand ton liner was originally Launch on May, 31st 1911 and completed on April, 1st 1912. Her performance at sea was tested on the second. Her il-fated maiden voyage Start on the tenth. On 11:40 PM, April 14, She hit an iceberg with 6 watertight compartments were flooded. She could still float wit 4 compartments flooded.
